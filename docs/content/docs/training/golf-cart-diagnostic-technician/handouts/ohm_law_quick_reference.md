# Student Handout — Ohm’s Law & Pack Math Quick Reference

## Formulas
- **V = I × R**  
- **I = V / R**  
- **R = V / I**  
- **P = V × I** (power in watts)

## Series vs Parallel (batteries)
| Connection | Voltage | Capacity (Ah) |
|---|---|---|
| Series | Adds | Same as one unit (approx.) |
| Parallel | Same as one unit | Adds |

### Common pack examples
- **36V:** 6 × 6V in series *(or other OEM arrangements — verify)*  
- **48V:** 6 × 8V in series *(common)* or 4 × 12V in series *(common)*  

## Meter reminders
| Measuring | Probe jacks | Parallel or series with circuit? |
|---|---|---|
| Voltage | V / COM | Parallel |
| Resistance / continuity | Ω / COM | Power removed; across component |
| Current | A / COM (fused) | Series — advanced; prefer clamp meter |

## Practice problems (answers upside-down section for self-check)
1. 48V across 16Ω → I = ?  
2. 24A through 2Ω → V = ?  
3. Six 8V batteries in series → V = ?

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1. 3A  
2. 48V  
3. 48V  

</details>
