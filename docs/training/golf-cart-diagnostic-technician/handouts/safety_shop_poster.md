# Shop Poster — Key Safety Reminders

**Post at bay entrance. Review Week 1; reinforce every session.**

1. Always wear appropriate PPE — no exceptions.  
2. Treat every battery pack as energized until verified zero with your meter.  
3. Never work alone on high-voltage or high-current systems during training.  
4. Remove all jewelry before working on electrical systems.  
5. Use one hand when possible when probing live circuits.  
6. Ventilate battery charging areas — hydrogen gas is explosive.  
7. Never bypass safety interlocks or HPD circuits for delivery.  
8. Document everything. If it isn’t written down, it didn’t happen.  
9. When in doubt — stop and ask. There are no stupid questions in safety.  
10. Gasoline work: extinguisher ready, ventilation on, spill control first.
