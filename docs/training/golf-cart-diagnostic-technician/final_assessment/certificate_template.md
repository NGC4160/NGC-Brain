## Issuance checklist
- [ ] Attendance ≥85% or makeups complete  
- [ ] Critical safety competencies signed  
- [ ] Final practical Pass  
- [ ] Written final ≥70%  
- [ ] Certificate ID logged in cohort roster (no student PII beyond name needed for shop records policy)
