# Student Handout — Resources & Further Learning

## OEM training pathways
- **Club Car** — dealer / authorized technician courses (contact regional rep)
- **EZ-GO (Textron)** — authorized service training
- **Yamaha Golf Car** — dealer technician programs  

Always obtain the **exact model-year service manual** for the cart being serviced.

## Controller / fault codes
- Curtis Instruments Fault Code Translator: https://faultcodes.curtisinstruments.com/
- Keep model-specific PDF charts in the shop binder

## Communities & media (use critically — verify against OEM)
- Forums: Cartaholics.com, GolfCartResource.com, Reddit r/golfcarts  
- YouTube: search brand-specific repair channels; validate steps against manuals  
- Battery University — lead-acid & lithium fundamentals  

## Program habits that beat random videos
1. Correct diagram first  
2. Measure with units  
3. 7-step process  
4. Diagnose before replace  
5. Never bypass HPD/interlocks for delivery  

## After graduation — suggested next skills
- OEM lithium/BMS tool certifications as available  
- Advanced soldering/connector repair  
- Fleet PM software / work-order systems used by your employer  
- Gas EFI diagnostics only with proper tooling and training
