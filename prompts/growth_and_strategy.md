# Growth & Strategy

Use when you want ideas, plans, or a second opinion:

---

**Help me grow NGC this quarter.**

Read `knowledge/`, `knowledge/08_finance/overview.md`, and `knowledge/09_daily_ops/improvement_backlog.md`.

Context:
- Current focus: [shop service + lithium / something else]
- Biggest pain right now: [e.g. not enough lithium leads, tech capacity, marketing ROI]
- Budget appetite: [low / medium / willing to invest]
- Time I can spend per week on growth: [hours]

Give me:
1. **Diagnosis** — 2–3 root causes based on what you know about the business
2. **30-day plan** — max 5 actions, ordered, with owner (Ryan/Christine/tech)
3. **Quick wins** — things we can do this week with no new software
4. **Metrics to track** — 3–5 numbers I should watch weekly
5. **What not to do** — distractions given we're not doing sales/rentals yet

End with: "Pick A, B, or C and I'll build the detailed playbook."

Offer three specific playbooks:
- **A)** Lithium lead generation
- **B)** Shop throughput & turnaround
- **C)** Marketing spend optimization
