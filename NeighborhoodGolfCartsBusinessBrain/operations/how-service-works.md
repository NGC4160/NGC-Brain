# How Service Works

## Step 1: Request service

Call **(985) 402-1206** or use the website contact form. Describe the cart issue or service needed.

## Step 2: Schedule and confirm

NGC schedules the earliest available pickup slot and sends confirmation by text and email. Customers can reschedule if the time does not work.

## Step 3: Pickup, repair, return

The team picks up the cart, transports it to the Covington facility, completes expert service, and returns it ready to ride.

## Typical timeline

Most jobs are completed within about one week from pickup to return, depending on parts and repair complexity.
