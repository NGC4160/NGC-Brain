# Pickup and Delivery

## Northshore

Free pickup and delivery for maintenance and repair within 40 miles of the Covington shop. Includes Mandeville, Madisonville, and Slidell.

## Southshore

Affordable flat-rate transport for New Orleans, Kenner, Metairie, and beyond. Same shop-quality service without the customer hauling a trailer.

## Customer presence

Customers do not need to be home for pickup as long as the team can access the cart.

## Goal

Eliminate transport hassle while delivering shop-level precision that driveway repairs cannot match.
