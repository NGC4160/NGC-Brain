# Service Area

Neighborhood Golf Carts serves the Northshore and Greater New Orleans area from Covington, LA.

## Northshore (free pickup and delivery within 40 miles)

- Covington
- Madisonville
- Mandeville
- Slidell
- Abita Springs
- Hammond
- Ponchatoula
- Lacombe

## Southshore (flat-rate transport)

- New Orleans
- Kenner
- Metairie
- River Parishes and surrounding areas

Customers outside the standard radius should still contact NGC — the team often travels further when needed.
