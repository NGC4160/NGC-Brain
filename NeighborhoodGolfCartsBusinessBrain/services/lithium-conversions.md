# Lithium Conversions

Professional LiFePO4 (Lithium Iron Phosphate) battery upgrades for golf carts.

## Why LiFePO4

- Non-combustible chemistry designed for golf cart use
- Suited to Louisiana heat and humidity
- Faster charging, more power, lighter weight than lead-acid
- Longer lifespan and lower maintenance

## Timeline

Most conversions complete in 3–5 business days once the cart is at the shop. End-to-end with pickup and delivery is typically about one week.
