# Preventive Maintenance

Routine tune-ups, inspections, and scheduled maintenance to keep carts reliable.

## Includes

- Regular tune-ups
- Inspections
- Routine maintenance for residential and commercial carts

## Free 7-point inspection (included with any service)

1. Wires, cables, and terminals
2. Battery distilled water levels (where applicable)
3. Lights and horn (if applicable)
4. Tire inflation, tread, and uneven wear
5. Steering and suspension
6. Wheel and steering wheel alignment
7. Brake operation
