# Diagnostics and Repairs

Shop-quality golf cart diagnostics and repairs with free Northshore pickup and delivery.

## Includes

- Computerized and electrical diagnostics
- Motor troubleshooting
- Complex system repairs
- Shop equipment not available for driveway/mobile work

## Process

NGC transports the cart to the Covington facility, performs repairs, and returns the cart in peak condition. Customers do not need to be home if the cart is accessible for pickup.

## Brands

Club Car, EZGO, Yamaha, and most major golf cart brands. Both gas and electric carts.
