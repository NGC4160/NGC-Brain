# Neighborhood Golf Carts — Business Brain Index

> **Neighborhood Golf Carts** is a Covington, Louisiana golf cart repair shop serving Southeast Louisiana with professional shop-based service and door-to-door pickup and delivery.

## Quick facts

- **Phone:** (985) 402-1206
- **Website:** https://www.ngcgolfcarts.com
- **Shop:** Professional facility in Covington, LA (St. Tammany Parish)
- **Founded:** 2023 (roots in neighborhood garage repairs since 2018)
- **Brands serviced:** Club Car, EZGO, Yamaha, and most major brands
- **Power types:** Gas and electric golf carts

## Service model

Shop-based repairs with valet logistics. Northshore customers within 40 miles of Covington receive free pickup and delivery. Southshore customers (New Orleans, Kenner, Metairie) use flat-rate transport.
