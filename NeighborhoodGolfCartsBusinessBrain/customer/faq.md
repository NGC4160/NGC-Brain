# Customer FAQ

## What areas do you serve?

Covington, Madisonville, Mandeville, Hammond, New Orleans, Kenner, Metairie, Slidell, Abita Springs, Ponchatoula, Lacombe, and surrounding Southeast Louisiana communities. Northshore customers within 40 miles of Covington get free pickup and delivery. Southshore customers use flat-rate transport.

## What services do you offer?

Golf cart repair, maintenance, and accessories including battery replacement, tire repair, brake service, electrical diagnostics, motor troubleshooting, lithium conversions, and routine maintenance — with pickup and delivery.

## How do I book an appointment?

Call **(985) 402-1206** or use the contact form at https://www.ngcgolfcarts.com/contact-us

## Do you charge for pickup?

Free within 40 miles of the Covington shop for Northshore customers. Flat-rate professional transport fee for Southshore customers in New Orleans, Kenner, and Metairie.

## What if I do not know what is wrong with my cart?

NGC offers diagnostic services to identify the issue and recommend the best repair path.

## Do you work on all golf cart brands?

Yes — Club Car, EZGO, Yamaha, and most major brands. Call to confirm if unsure.

## Do you work on gas and electric carts?

Yes. Technicians service both gas-powered and electric golf carts.

## How long do lithium conversions take?

Typically 3–5 business days in shop, about one week total with pickup and delivery.

## Is lithium safe for neighborhood use?

Yes. NGC installs LiFePO4 (Lithium Iron Phosphate), designed for stability and safety in golf cart applications.
