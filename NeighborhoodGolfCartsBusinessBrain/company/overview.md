# Company Overview

**Neighborhood Golf Carts** provides expert golf cart repair, maintenance, battery service, lithium conversions, and custom upgrades from a professional shop in Covington, Louisiana.

## Value proposition

- Shop-level diagnostics and repairs (not driveway-limited mobile work)
- Door-to-door pickup and delivery so customers never need a trailer
- Free Northshore pickup and delivery within 40 miles of Covington
- Flat-rate transport for Southshore customers
- Certified technicians on all major brands
- Transparent pricing and clear communication
- Typical turnaround about one week from pickup to return

## Customers

- Residential golf cart owners
- Commercial fleet managers
- Neighborhoods, golf courses, and properties with multiple carts

## Differentiators

- Locally owned in Covington, LA
- LiFePO4 lithium conversions (non-combustible chemistry suited to Louisiana heat)
- Free 7-point inspection included with any service
- Customers do not need to be home for pickup if the cart is accessible
