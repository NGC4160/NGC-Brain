# Company Story

## Origins (2018)

After moving to a Madisonville neighborhood and buying a golf cart, the founders began fixing carts for neighbors out of their garage. Local owners struggled to find reliable, professional service.

## Launch (2023)

Neighborhood Golf Carts launched as a formal business in 2023 to fill the service gap across Southeast Louisiana.

## Evolution to shop-based service

As demand grew, the business moved from mobile-only repairs to a fully equipped professional facility in Covington. Shop-based work enables deeper diagnostics, lithium conversions, and heavy-duty repairs that are not practical in a driveway.

Pickup and delivery were retained so customers still get door-to-door convenience without hauling carts themselves.
