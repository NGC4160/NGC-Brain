# SOP-44 — Failed Pickup / Customer Not Home

**Owner:** Driver (Roy) → Christine  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Status:** Draft — ready for review

## Purpose

Handle missed pickups without silent failures.

## Steps

1. Attempt contact on site / phone once.
2. Photo door/gate or note why failed (no PII beyond job need).
3. Do not leave cart unsecured on street.
4. Notify Christine same day; reschedule.
5. Job note: `Failed pickup YYYY-MM-DD — reason — retry ___`.
6. Fee policy: SM decides if second trip charges again.
