# SOP-21 — 7-Point Safety Inspection

**Owner:** Assigned technician  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Status:** Draft — ready for review

## Purpose

Perform the **free** 7-point safety inspection with every service.

## Checklist

1. Wires, cables, terminals
2. Battery water levels (lead-acid) / pack condition (lithium)
3. Lights and horn
4. Tires — inflation, tread, wear
5. Steering and suspension
6. Drivetrain (axle & motor)
7. Brakes

## Rules

- Note fails on job; photo defects (SOP-29).
- Unsafe to drive → do not release; flag Ryan/Christine.
- Repeat at QC if major work done (SOP-28).

## Done when

Checklist noted on job; unsafe items escalated.
