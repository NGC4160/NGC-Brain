# SOP-41 — Pickup from Customer

**Owner:** Driver (Roy)  
**Handoff to:** Office Coordinator (Christine)  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Applies to:** All NGC locations  
**Status:** Draft — ready for review

## Purpose

Pick up the cart safely, document condition, and get it into shop intake without missing keys, chargers, or damage notes.

## Steps

1. Confirm address, zone fee, and invoice # before leaving.
2. On arrival: photos of cart (front, sides, battery area if accessible), note existing damage.
3. Collect keys, chargers, loose accessories — bag and label with invoice #.
4. Load/secure per SOP-43.
5. On return: deliver cart to INTAKE; give Christine keys/bag + condition notes.
6. Christine: create/update HCP notes + board card (SOP-11 / SOP-20).

## Done when

Cart on lot, intake owns it, photos/notes attached, fee correct.
