# SOP-01 — Customer Intake

**Owner:** Office Coordinator (Christine)  
**Backup:** Service Manager (Ryan)  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Applies to:** All NGC locations (zone fees from Location Profile)  
**Status:** Live — staff draft captured 2026-07-12

## Purpose

Respond quickly to every lead, gather what the shop needs for an accurate estimate, and set clear expectations on diagnostics and pickup/delivery — without over-chasing leads.

## When to use

Any new lead or inbound request: web form, text, call, email, social DM, or walk-in that needs intake into the system.

## Prerequisites

- Access to CRM / Housecall Pro (customer account, jobs, photos, notes)
- Diagnostic terms template ready to send
- Current pickup/delivery zones (see Location Profile / [shop_services.md](../03_services/shop_services.md))

## Steps

### 1. Initial response (same day, immediately)

On any new lead, send this text right away:

> Hi, this is [Name] with Neighborhood Golf Carts. We’ve just received your request about your golf cart. Do you have time for a quick call so we can get you an accurate estimate?

**Do not** quote prices, promise turnaround, or schedule a bay until after the call (or a complete text Q&A that covers Steps 3–5).

### 2. No-response follow-up

| Timing | Action |
|--------|--------|
| No reply after **24 hours** | Send one follow-up: *Just checking back on your request. Still a good time to chat?* |
| Still no reply | **Stop all outreach.** Do not send a third message. |

Mark the lead/job accordingly (e.g. no response / closed lost) so it does not clog the waitlist.

### 3. On the phone

1. Identify the customer’s problem and how NGC can help (diagnostic vs known-scope estimate vs lithium/upgrade).
2. **Diagnostics:** Quote **$179 plus tax** (minimum service charge; not waived; applies toward repair on known-issue jobs when that policy applies — see [shop_services.md](../03_services/shop_services.md)).
3. Ask **location** to set pickup & delivery expectations:

| Zone | Fee |
|------|-----|
| Inside **40 miles** of the North Shore shop **or anywhere on the South Shore of Lake Pontchartrain** | **Free** |
| Outside that free zone | **$99** pickup & delivery |

4. Confirm drop-off vs Roy pickup preference once zone/fee is clear.
5. Do **not** book a lithium start or firm drop-off date if shop WIP is over limit — park on waitlist for Service Manager (see SOP-02 / [shop_throughput.md](shop_throughput.md)).

### 4. Information collection

Collect everything required for the online / CRM customer form, plus:

- Detailed **symptoms** (diagnostics), or
- Clear **scope of work** (estimates / upgrades)

Enough detail that the Service Manager can estimate without another discovery call when possible.

### 5. Pictures required

**Diagnostics — always request:**

1. Front of the cart  
2. Whole battery compartment  
3. Whole dashboard  
4. Any data tags  

**Estimates — request:**

1. Front of the cart  
2. Whole battery compartment  
3. Any data tags  
4. Specific photos of the area/part being repaired or upgraded (broken bracket, damaged switch, etc.)  
5. Inspiration photos if they want lifted tires or other cosmetic upgrades  

**Goal:** Give the Service Manager enough detail to identify the cart model and build an accurate estimate.

### 6. After the call

1. Attach all received photos to the **customer’s account**.
2. Add **detailed notes** in the system (complaint, zone/fee quoted, drop-off vs pickup, tax mentioned on diagnostic).
3. **Diagnostics:** Office Coordinator sends **diagnostic terms**. When the customer approves, the system moves the job to the **waitlist** for the Service Manager to schedule.
4. **Estimates:** Leave the job in the system for the Service Manager to create the estimate.
5. **Who sends estimates:** Office Coordinator sends all estimates **except** those that require the Service Manager to create them first.

## Done when (exit criteria)

| Path | Done when |
|------|-----------|
| Diagnostic | Terms sent; on approval → waitlist; photos + notes attached |
| Estimate | Photos + notes attached; job queued for SM estimate (or OC-sent estimate if already created) |
| No reply | One follow-up sent after 24h; outreach stopped; lead not on active waitlist |

## Exceptions

| Situation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Walk-in with cart already on trailer | Same info + photo standards; may skip “quick call” text; still collect form fields and zone |
| Customer only wants lithium package price | Use Professional Kit quote path (SOP-04); still collect location, photos, deposit language |
| Outside free P&D zone and customer refuses $99 | Offer customer drop-off/pickup at shop; do not waive fee without Service Manager approval |
| Capacity full / WIP over limit | Do not promise a start date; waitlist for Service Manager |
| Comeback / warranty | Use complaint path (SOP-08) — do not re-quote full diagnostic as a new lead without checking history |

## Scripts (copy/paste)

**Initial**

> Hi, this is [Name] with Neighborhood Golf Carts. We’ve just received your request about your golf cart. Do you have time for a quick call so we can get you an accurate estimate?

**24-hour follow-up**

> Just checking back on your request. Still a good time to chat?

**Diagnostic fee (spoken)**

> Our diagnostic and inspection is $179 plus tax. That gets the cart on the bench so we can find the issue and give you a real repair plan.

**Free P&D**

> Pickup and delivery is free if you’re within 40 miles of our North Shore shop, or anywhere on the South Shore of Lake Pontchartrain.

**Paid P&D**

> Outside that area, pickup and delivery is a flat $99.

From Location Profile: shop address, free-zone definition, paid flat fee, phone/text number used for outreach.
