# SOP-53 — Min/Max Stocking (Fast Movers)

**Owner:** Service Manager  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Status:** Draft — ready for review

## Purpose

Keep fast movers in stock without overbuying.

## Steps

1. Maintain list on Location Profile (cables, common wear, consumables).
2. Set min/max with Ryan; review monthly.
3. Reorder at min; do not use job deposits to quietly restock personal preference.
4. Lithium kits: stock policy is SM decision (cash vs SLA).
