# SOP-42 — Delivery to Customer

**Owner:** Driver (Roy)  
**Confirm payment with:** Office Coordinator (Christine)  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Applies to:** All NGC locations  
**Status:** Draft — ready for review

## Purpose

Deliver only when payment/terms are clear; hand off lithium care correctly; document delivery.

## Steps

1. Before leaving: Christine confirms balance collected or SM release OK (SOP-07).
2. Load from READY; bring care guide if lithium (SOP-33).
3. On site: unload safely (SOP-43); brief customer; lithium charger-only reminder.
4. Capture delivery confirmation (photo / signature per shop practice).
5. Report complete to Christine → HCP Complete, card off board.

## Done when

Cart delivered, customer briefed, job closable.
