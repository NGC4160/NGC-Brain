# SOP-73 — Pricebook Change Control

**Owner:** Service Manager (Ryan)  
**Editor support:** Christine  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Status:** Draft — ready for review

## Purpose

Only controlled edits to HCP prices — never invent prices in quotes.

## Rules

- Ryan approves new/changed prices.
- Deactivate legacy: mobile, trip charges, NGC Conversion (legacy_pricebook_cleanup.md).
- After edits: export + sync (SOP-72).
- Quotes use Professional Kits and current shop lines only.
- Log material price changes in decision_log.md.
