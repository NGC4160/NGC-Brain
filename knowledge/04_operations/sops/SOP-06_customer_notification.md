# SOP-06 — Customer Notification (Status / Ready / Delay)

**Owner:** Office Coordinator (Christine)  
**Backup:** Service Manager (Ryan)  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Status:** Draft — ready for review

## Purpose

Keep customers informed without over-texting — especially when carts hit READY or are delayed.

## When to use

Status change that matters: estimate ready, deposit needed, parts delay, READY, delay past promise, Roy ETA.

## Rules

- Use HCP text/email; no customer phones in shared shop sheets.
- READY: notify **same day** QC passes.
- Delay: notify as soon as ETA slips (parts or WIP).

## Templates

**Ready:** Your golf cart is ready at Neighborhood Golf Carts. Reply here or call 985-402-1206 to schedule pickup or delivery.

**Deposit:** We need your $[amount] deposit before we can order parts. Payment link: [link]

**Delay:** Quick update — your cart is waiting on [parts/approval]. New target: [day]. Call us with questions: 985-402-1206.

**Estimate ready:** Your estimate is ready in the link we sent.

## Done when

Customer notified; job note `Notified YYYY-MM-DD — [reason]`.
