# SOP-45 — Zone Map & Fee Exception Log

**Owner:** Service Manager (Ryan)  
**Logged by:** Christine / Roy  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Applies to:** Covington until zip/map finalized  
**Status:** Draft — ready for review

## Purpose

Document every P&D fee exception or edge-case address so quoting stays consistent and the eventual zone map is accurate.

## Standard fees

Free: within 40 mi North Shore shop. **$99:** South Shore or outside 40 mi.

## When to log

- Unclear which zone (parish edge, island, mixed)
- Fee waived or reduced (SM only)
- Customer disputes zone
- Quoted free but should be $99 (or reverse)

## Log format (shared sheet or HCP note — no unnecessary PII)

`P&D exception YYYY-MM-DD | invoice # | zone called | fee charged | reason | approved by`

## Steps

1. Spot exception → do not invent a new permanent rule on the fly.
2. SM decides fee for that job.
3. Log line above; charge invoice correctly.
4. Monthly: Ryan reviews log → update Location Profile / map.

## Done when

Exception documented and invoice matches decision.
