# SOP-24 — Estimate Handoff to Front Office

**Owner:** Assigned technician → Service Manager → Office Coordinator  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Status:** Draft — ready for review

## Purpose

Turn bay findings into a customer estimate without ordering parts early.

## Steps

1. Tech writes findings, likely parts, labor hours, photos (SOP-29).
2. Ryan reviews / builds HCP estimate with pricebook lines.
3. Christine sends estimate (SOP-03 / SOP-04 rules).
4. Card: DIAG or PARTS with `Waiting approval` — **not** IN REPAIR.
5. On approval: deposit if needed (SOP-05) then IN REPAIR.

## Done when

Estimate sent or queued; no premature parts order.
