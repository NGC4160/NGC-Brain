# SOP-12 — WIP Limits & Stop-Scheduling Rule

**Owner:** Service Manager (Ryan)  
**Enforced daily by:** Office Coordinator (Christine)  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Applies to:** All NGC locations  
**Status:** Draft — ready for review

## Purpose

Protect turnaround by **stopping new intake** when the shop is overloaded — especially lithium SLA.

## When to use

- Morning huddle sets stoplight red (SOP-10)
- WIP lanes 2–5 exceed **6** any time during day
- Lithium in progress exceeds **4**

## Hard rules

| Limit | Max | Action when over |
|-------|----:|------------------|
| Active WIP (DIAG+PARTS+REPAIR+QC) | **6** | Stop new drop-offs |
| Lithium in progress | **4** | Pause new lithium starts |
| Needs scheduling | **5** | Clear queue before adding more leads to schedule |
| Unassigned active cards | **0** after 8:30 | Assign before taking more work |

## Stop-scheduling procedure

1. Ryan declares **RED** on board header (or Christine sees count ≥7).
2. Christine: no new drop-off bookings; offers waitlist only.
3. Finish list becomes mandatory focus (SOP-10).
4. Resume yellow/green only when WIP ≤6 and aged lithium is under control.
5. Job note on declined/delayed booking: `Capacity hold YYYY-MM-DD — waitlist`.

## Customer language

> We’re protecting our turnaround times for carts already in the shop. We can put you on the waitlist and call as soon as a bay opens.

## Done when

No new arrivals scheduled under red; waitlist is honest; stoplight updated when WIP drops.
