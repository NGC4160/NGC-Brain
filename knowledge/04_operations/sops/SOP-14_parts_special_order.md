# SOP-14 — Parts Special-Order Process

**Owner:** Service Manager (Ryan)  
**Deposit gate:** Christine (SOP-05)  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Status:** Draft — ready for review

## Purpose

Order special parts only after deposit, track ETA, and release to the bay without losing the job in PARTS forever.

## Flow

1. Estimate approved → SOP-05 deposit collected.
2. Ryan (or designee) places vendor order (SOP-52).
3. Job note: `Ordered YYYY-MM-DD — vendor — ETA ___ — PO/ref ___`.
4. Board stays **PARTS** with Parts? unchecked until received.
5. Receive per SOP-50 → check Parts? → move to IN REPAIR when bay ready.
6. If ETA slips → Christine notifies customer (SOP-06).

## Done when

Parts received and tied to invoice #; card can leave PARTS.
