# SOP-13 — End-of-Day Lane Audit

**Owner:** Service Manager (Ryan)  
**Support:** Christine (READY/OUT TODAY), techs (their cards)  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Applies to:** All NGC locations  
**Status:** Draft — ready for review

## Purpose

Close the day with honest lanes in HCP and on the board so tomorrow’s huddle starts clean.

## When to use

Every operating day, **~4:45 PM** (≈5 minutes).

## Steps

1. Walk the board — every open job has a card in the correct lane.
2. DIAG / IN REPAIR / QC: tech assigned or move to PARTS/READY/INTAKE as appropriate — **no blank tech overnight**.
3. Match HCP notes to board (SOP-11 sync table).
4. OUT TODAY: carts gone → HCP **Complete**, remove cards; still here → back to READY.
5. Update header WIP for tomorrow.
6. Note blockers (parts ETA, deposit, customer approval) on job.
7. Policy change today? Log in decision_log.md.

## Done when

Board = floor truth; HCP not lying; zero unassigned active cards overnight.
