# SOP-20 — Cart Receive & Bay Setup

**Owner:** Assigned tech / intake handoff from Christine or Roy  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Status:** Draft — ready for review

## Purpose

Get every cart tagged, photographed, and complaint-confirmed before diagnosis or repair.

## Steps

1. Match cart to invoice # / board card.
2. Place tag or bag keys with invoice #.
3. Restate complaint in HCP note (customer words + tech observation).
4. Intake photos if missing (SOP-01 / SOP-29).
5. Move card INTAKE → DIAG (or PARTS if deposit/parts first).
6. Begin SOP-21 7-point as part of service.

## Done when

Card in correct lane, tech assigned, notes/photos started.
