# SOP-40 — Daily Route Build & Zone Batching

**Owner:** Driver (Roy)  
**Support:** Office Coordinator (Christine)  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Applies to:** Covington / Location Profile zones  
**Status:** Draft — ready for review

## Purpose

Batch pickups and deliveries by zone so Roy isn’t running one-off trips all day — and fees match policy.

## Zone fees (Covington)

| Zone | Fee |
|------|-----|
| Within 40 miles of North Shore shop | **Free** |
| South Shore of Lake Pontchartrain | **$99** |
| Outside 40 miles | **$99** |

## Steps

1. After huddle, take **OUT TODAY** + scheduled pickups from Christine.
2. Group by zone (North Shore free vs paid South Shore / outside).
3. Sequence route to minimize backtracking; paid-zone trips batched together when possible.
4. Confirm each job has fee quoted correctly on invoice (SOP-45 if exception).
5. Execute SOP-41 (pickup) / SOP-42 (delivery); trailer safety SOP-43.
6. End of day: report fails to Christine (SOP-44).

## Done when

Day’s list complete or exceptions logged; no unpaid $99 trips “forgotten.”
