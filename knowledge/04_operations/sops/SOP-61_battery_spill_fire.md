# SOP-61 — Battery Handling & Spill / Fire Response

**Owner:** All shop staff  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Status:** Draft — ready for review

## Purpose

Handle lead-acid and LiFePO4 safely; respond to spills/incidents.

## Lead-acid

- Support packs when lifting; avoid shorting terminals
- Neutralizer / spill kit for acid; PPE on
- Ventilate; no smoking/sparks near charging lead-acid

## LiFePO4

- Use correct lithium charger only
- Do not crush, puncture, or reverse polarity
- If pack damaged/smoking: evacuate area, call emergency services if needed, notify Ryan — do not throw water casually on electrical fires without proper extinguisher type

## Spill steps

1. PPE on 2. Contain 3. Neutralize acid per kit 4. Dispose per rules 5. Log incident
