# SOP-33 — Lithium QC + Customer Care Handoff

**Owner:** Tech (QC) + Christine/Roy (handoff)  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Status:** Draft — ready for review

## Purpose

Close lithium jobs with function verified and customer care understood.

## QC

- Full function + test drive (SOP-28)
- Charger powers; monitor reads; accessories via DC-DC OK
- Care guide printed/ready

## Customer points (say or give guide)

- Charge with **lithium-specific charger only** — never lead-acid charger
- Target 20–30% min SOC normal use; 50% min in freezing weather
- No watering or equalization
- Do not charge below 32°F (0°C)
- 5-year battery + BMS warranty

## Done when

Care guide given; tracker boxes complete; READY notified.
