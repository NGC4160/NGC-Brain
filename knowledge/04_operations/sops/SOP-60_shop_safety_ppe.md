# SOP-60 — Shop Safety & PPE

**Owner:** Service Manager (Ryan) — all staff comply  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Status:** Draft — ready for review

## Purpose

Daily hazard control in a battery/cart shop.

## Expectations

- Eye protection when grinding, battery work, or flying debris risk
- Gloves appropriate to task (acid vs cut)
- Closed-toe shoes; no loose clothing near rotating parts
- Lift with help / equipment — carts are heavy
- Keep aisles clear; no open acid on walk paths
- Know extinguisher and eyewash/spill kit locations (SOP-61)

## Incidents

Report injuries/near misses to Ryan same day; document.
