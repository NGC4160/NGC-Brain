# SOP-15 — Job Prioritization (Lithium vs Repair)

**Owner:** Service Manager (Ryan)  
**Used in:** Morning huddle finish list (SOP-10)  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Status:** Draft — ready for review

## Priority order (default)

1. Safety / immobilized comebacks SM flagged urgent
2. Lithium Day 3+ (SLA breach risk)
3. Lithium Day 2 with parts in hand
4. READY / customer waiting on lot
5. Repairs with parts in hand and aged >5–7 days
6. New diagnostics
7. New lithium Day 0–1 (only if capacity)

## Rules

- Do not start new lithium while Day 3+ Li sits (unless SM documents exception).
- Parts-blocked jobs stay PARTS — they don’t steal bay time from finish list.
- Peyton blocks are scheduled, not forever WIP.
