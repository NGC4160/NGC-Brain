# SOP-63 — Shop Open & Close Checklist

**Owner:** Opening/closing staff (Ryan or designee)  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Status:** Draft — ready for review

## Open

- [ ] Unlock; lights; HVAC as needed
- [ ] Board visible; glance WIP
- [ ] Christine desk systems up
- [ ] No overnight hazards (leaks, unsecured carts)

## Close

- [ ] EOD lane audit done or Ryan aware (SOP-13)
- [ ] Tools put away (SOP-62)
- [ ] Batteries/chargers safe state
- [ ] Doors locked; keys secured
- [ ] Optional: board photo for remote SM
