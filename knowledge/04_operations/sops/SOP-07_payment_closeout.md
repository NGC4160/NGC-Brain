# SOP-07 — Payment Collection & Closeout

**Owner:** Office Coordinator (Christine)  
**Backup:** Service Manager (Ryan)  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Applies to:** All NGC locations  
**Status:** Draft — ready for review

## Purpose

Collect the balance, close the HCP job cleanly, and release the cart only when money (or SM-approved terms) is settled.

## When to use

Cart in READY or OUT TODAY; customer pickup or Roy delivery; any final invoice.

## Hard rules

| Do | Do not |
|----|--------|
| Collect balance before release (or SM written OK) | Release cart with silent unpaid balance |
| Send invoice / pay link when remote | Assume Venmo/Zelle posted without checking |
| Complete HCP when cart gone | Leave READY ghosts on the board |

## Payment methods

Cash, card, check, Venmo, Zelle — confirm current preferred methods with Christine. Prefer HCP card link for audit trail.

## Steps

1. Confirm final invoice (repairs + tax + P&D + cores − deposits).
2. Collect payment or send pay link; note method/date on job.
3. Lithium: confirm care guide given (SOP-33).
4. Customer pickup or Roy delivery (SOP-42).
5. HCP **Complete**; remove board card (SOP-11).
6. Thank-you / review ask if process allows (Admin Bot Phase 2 later).

## Done when

Paid (or SM terms); cart off lot; HCP Complete; card removed.
