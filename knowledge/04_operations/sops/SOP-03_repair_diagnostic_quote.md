# SOP-03 — Quote & Estimate (Repair / Diagnostic)

**Owner:** Office Coordinator (Christine) — sends quotes and diagnostic terms  
**Backup / creates repair estimates:** Service Manager (Ryan)  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Applies to:** All NGC locations (prices from company pricebook)  
**Status:** Draft — ready for Christine pilot

## Purpose

Quote diagnostics and repairs the same way every time: **$179 minimum is never waived**, known-issue work still carries the minimum toward the repair, photos support accurate estimates, and no mobile or invented prices.

## When to use

Customer has a repair, noise, no-go, battery, brake, suspension, electrical, or “something’s wrong” request — anything that is **not** a straight Professional lithium package (use SOP-04 for that).

## Prerequisites

- Intake complete or in progress (SOP-01): problem description, location for P&D, photos
- Access to HCP pricebook / [pricebook_reference.md](../../03_services/pricebook_reference.md)
- Diagnostic terms template ready (Office Coordinator sends)

## Hard rules (non-negotiable)

| Do | Do not |
|----|--------|
| Quote **$179 + tax** for shop diagnostic / minimum service charge | Waive, “credit later,” or skip the $179 to win the job |
| On **known-issue** jobs, say $179 **applies toward the repair** | Pretend known-issue work is free to look at |
| Use **HCP / pricebook** line items for parts & labor | Invent prices or guess from memory |
| Shop-only language | Quote mobile, on-site, or trip charges |
| Request photos before firm repair estimates when possible | Promise a fixed repair total with no photos / no inspection when the issue is unclear |

## Path A vs Path B (choose on the call)

| Path | When | What you quote first |
|------|------|----------------------|
| **A — Full diagnostic** | Cause unknown (won’t move, intermittent, electrical mystery, “not sure”) | **Diagnostic & Inspection $179 + tax** |
| **B — Known-issue / scoped repair** | Clear problem (broken A-arm, flat tire, obvious damaged part, customer wants specific R&R) | **$179 minimum service charge** (applies toward repair) + estimate of repair lines when photos/SM allow |

Both paths include the **free 7-point safety inspection** with service.

## Steps

### 1. Classify the job (Path A or B)

1. Listen for symptom vs known broken part.
2. If unclear → **Path A (diagnostic $179)**.
3. If obvious scoped work → **Path B** (still explain $179 minimum toward repair).
4. If they want lithium → hand off to **SOP-04** (may still need diagnostic first if cart has other faults).

### 2. Collect what the estimator needs

Follow SOP-01 photo rules:

**Diagnostics:** front of cart, whole battery compartment, whole dashboard, data tags.  
**Estimates:** those plus close-ups of the damaged area / part; inspiration photos for cosmetic upgrades.

Also capture: make/model if known, gas vs electric, when it started, any recent work.

### 3. Quote the money clearly (phone)

**Path A — diagnostic**

> Our shop diagnostic and inspection is $179 plus tax. That gets the cart on the bench so we can find the issue and give you a real repair plan. Every service includes a free 7-point safety inspection.

**Path B — known issue**

> Even when we know what’s wrong, there’s a $179 minimum service charge — and that amount goes toward the repair. I’ll get you an estimate for the parts and labor once we have photos / the cart in.

**Batteries**

> A complete [6V/8V/12V] battery replacement with install is $[price] plus tax. We need an $800 deposit before we order the batteries. Core charges apply when the old batteries come off.

**Do not say:** “We’ll waive the diagnostic if you repair with us” / “Inspection is free” (7-point is free; diagnostic is not) / “We can come to you.”

### 4. Who creates vs who sends

| Situation | Creates estimate / terms | Sends to customer |
|-----------|--------------------------|-------------------|
| Diagnostic terms (Path A) | Template / OC | **Office Coordinator** |
| Simple known-issue with clear pricebook lines + photos | SM (or OC if SM authorized that pattern) | **Office Coordinator** |
| Complex electrical, controller, intermittent, multi-system | **Service Manager** | OC after SM creates |
| Battery package (standard sets) | OC or SM using pricebook | **Office Coordinator** |

Per SOP-01: OC sends all estimates **except** those that still need the Service Manager to create them first.

### 5. After the quote / terms

**Path A — diagnostic**

1. OC sends **diagnostic terms**.
2. On customer approval → job moves to **waitlist** for Service Manager to schedule.
3. Photos + notes attached to customer account (SOP-01).
4. Do not schedule a bay if WIP is over limit — waitlist (SOP-02 / shop throughput).

**Path B — repair estimate**

1. Leave job for SM to create estimate when required; OC sends when ready.
2. If parts (batteries, motors, controllers, special order) → **SOP-05** deposit before order.
3. Job note: `Repair quote sent YYYY-MM-DD` or `Diag terms sent YYYY-MM-DD`.

### 6. After diagnosis (shop finds the issue)

1. Tech / SM findings → estimate with pricebook lines (SOP-24 handoff).
2. OC sends repair estimate.
3. Customer approves → schedule repair; collect deposits if ordering parts (SOP-05).
4. Remind: $179 diagnostic already paid **applies toward** agreed repair when that was the Path B / known-issue policy — for Path A, follow how the job was written in HCP (diagnostic line vs credit toward repair per SM).

*Default customer language after Path A:* diagnostic fee paid for finding the problem; repair is additional per estimate unless SM notes a credit on that job.

*Default customer language on Path B known-issue:* the $179 minimum **goes toward** the repair total.

## Send checklist (every repair / diag quote)

- [ ] Path A or B chosen and explained  
- [ ] **$179 + tax** stated (never waived)  
- [ ] Path B: “applies toward repair” said  
- [ ] Photos requested / attached  
- [ ] P&D fee for their zone  
- [ ] Shop-only — no mobile  
- [ ] Pricebook SKUs only (no legacy mobile / trip / NGC Conversion lines)  
- [ ] Deposit mentioned if batteries / motor / controller / special order  
- [ ] Free 7-point mentioned with service  
- [ ] Job notes updated  

## Scripts — text templates

**Diagnostic terms summary**

> Hi — Neighborhood Golf Carts here. Shop diagnostic & inspection is $179 + tax. That covers getting your cart on the bench to find the issue and a free 7-point safety inspection. Pickup/delivery: [free / $99]. Reply to approve and we’ll add you to the schedule waitlist.

**Known-issue minimum**

> Even with a known issue, our minimum service charge is $179 + tax, and that amount goes toward the repair. We’ll send the parts/labor estimate once [we have your photos / the cart is here].

**After estimate ready**

> Your repair estimate is ready in the link we sent. Questions? Call 985-402-1206. If we need to order [batteries/parts], a deposit is required before ordering.

## Done when (exit criteria)

| Path | Done when |
|------|-----------|
| Diagnostic | Terms sent; on approval → waitlist; photos/notes attached |
| Repair estimate | Estimate created/sent with pricebook lines; deposit path started if needed |
| Battery quote | Price + $800 deposit + cores + P&D explained; handoff to SOP-05 on approval |
| Cannot quote yet | Photos/SM review requested; customer knows the next step |

## Exceptions

| Situation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Customer demands waived diagnostic | Decline politely; SM only for any one-off exception (rare, documented on job) |
| Comeback / warranty | SOP-08 — do not auto-charge full new diagnostic without checking history / SM |
| “Just tell me a price over the phone” with unknown cause | Quote $179 diagnostic; do not guess repair total |
| Pricebook shows mobile / trip / legacy line | Do not use — flag for cleanup |
| Fleet / HOA | SM sets terms; OC sends |
| Advanced / intermittent after standard diag | Peyton trigger per shop throughput — do not promise Peyton on every job |
