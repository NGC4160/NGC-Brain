# SOP-50 — Receiving & Put-Away

**Owner:** Service Manager / designated receiver  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Status:** Draft — ready for review

## Purpose

Match inbound parts to jobs and put them where the bay can find them.

## Steps

1. Match packing slip to PO / invoice #.
2. Label parts with invoice # immediately.
3. Note received + date on HCP job; check Parts? on board.
4. Touch QBO inventory when that is the shop practice (Jill/Ryan).
5. Notify tech/Ryan parts ready → can leave PARTS lane.
