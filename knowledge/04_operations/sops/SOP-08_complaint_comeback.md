# SOP-08 — Complaint / Comeback Intake

**Owner:** Office Coordinator (Christine)  
**Disposition:** Service Manager (Ryan)  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-12  
**Status:** Draft — ready for review

## Purpose

Log comebacks fairly — fix real issues without automatic free rework or blind new full diagnostics.

## Steps

1. Listen; capture symptom, date of prior work, invoice # if known.
2. Do **not** promise free repair or waived diagnostic on the call.
3. Create/ reopen job; note `COMEBACK — prior invoice #___`.
4. Photos if useful (SOP-01).
5. Ryan decides: warranty, goodwill, paid diagnostic, or paid repair.
6. Schedule per capacity (SOP-02); usually DIAG lane (SOP-11).

## Done when

Comeback logged, SM disposition set, customer given next step.
